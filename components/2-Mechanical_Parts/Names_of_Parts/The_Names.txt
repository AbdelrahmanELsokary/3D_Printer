1- Printer Frame (Aluminum / Acrylic / 3D-printed) – Main structure.

2- Smooth Rods (8mm) – For linear movement along axes.

3- Linear Bearings (LM8UU) – Slide along the smooth rods.

4- Lead Screws / Threaded Rods – For Z-axis movement.

5- GT2 Timing Belts – Rubber belts for X and Y movement.

6- GT2 Pulleys – Gear wheels for driving the belts.

7- Idler Pulleys / Bearings – Guide and tension the belts.

8- Bed Leveling Springs – Used under the bed for manual leveling.
